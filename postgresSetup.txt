##Here's how to set up your local postgres account
Resources: https://www.digitalocean.com/community/tutorials/how-to-install-and-use-postgresql-on-ubuntu-18-04

$sudo -i -u postgres (switches you to a postgres user)

$psql (gets you access to the postgres cli)

\q (quits the prompt)

$sudo -u postgres psql (gets you access to the prompt without switching the user)
